Homemade Fried Rice

Here is our basic recipe for classic Chinese-American restaurant style fried rice. This recipe conveniently serves as a flexible template for you to sub in your preferred protein and quick-cooking vegetables.

Ingredients

  • ½ cup frozen peas (defrosted)
  • ½ cup frozen corn (defrosted)
  • 3 cups long grain rice (cooked)
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 garlic clove, minced
  • 1 cup cooked cut meat
  • 2 large eggs, lightly beaten
  • 1 teaspoon minced ginger

Steps

  1. Heat a tablespoon of your preferred oil in a pan. Pour in eggs, swirl the pan so the eggs form a large thin pancake. As soon the egg has set, turn it out the pan on to a cutting board. Cool, cut into 1 inch pieces.
  2. Add a ½ teaspoon of oil to the pan. Stir fry the garlic and ginger for 1 minute. Pour in soy sauce, stir fry rice in pan, stirring occasionally until oil has absorbed.
  3. Add vegetables in hot pan and cook for 1-2 minutes. Combine meat and egg piece to mixture. Turn out and serve mixture in a bowl.
