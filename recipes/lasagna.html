Homemade Lasagna

Here is the recipe for our super-meaty lasagna, courtesy of Chef John. For the sauce, we will use 50-50 Italian sausage and lean ground beef, paired with our finest ricotta filling.

By Lex Riley

Ingredients

Meat Sauce
  • 1 ½ pounds lean ground beef
  • 1 pound bulk Italian sausage
  • 8 ounces fresh mushroom, coarsely chopped
  • ½ teaspoon black pepper
  • ¼ teaspoon chili flakes
  • 6 cups marinara sauce
Pasta
  • 1 package lasagna noodles
Cheese Filling
  • 2 pounds ricotta cheese
  • ⅔ cup freshly grated Parmigiano-Reggiano
  • 2 large eggs
  • 8 ounces fresh mozzarella cheese
